About Casa Marco Museum

Our mission, history, and dedication to art and culture

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Casa Marco Museum has been a beacon of culture and education for over four decades. Our mission is to preserve and share the world's artistic and historical treasures with visitors from around the globe.

What began as a small private collection has grown into one of the region's most prestigious cultural institutions, housing over 50,000 artifacts and artworks spanning 5,000 years of human history.

Our Mission

Casa Marco Museum is dedicated to:

  • Preserving cultural heritage for future generations
  • Making art and history accessible to all
  • Fostering education and cultural understanding
  • Supporting research and scholarship
  • Creating engaging and inspiring visitor experiences
