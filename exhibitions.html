Current Exhibitions

Explore our curated collections and special displays

Current

Renaissance Masters

January 15 - June 30, 2025 | Gallery A

Step into the world of Renaissance art and witness the masterpieces that defined an era. This exhibition features works from the greatest minds of the Renaissance period, including Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael.

Highlights:

  • Leonardo's scientific sketches and studies
  • Michelangelo's sculptural designs
  • Raphael's portrait collection
  • Interactive timeline of Renaissance innovations
Featured

Modern Impressions

March 1 - August 15, 2025 | Gallery B

Discover the revolutionary art movement that broke away from traditional techniques and captured fleeting moments of light and color. Experience works by Monet, Renoir, Degas, and other Impressionist pioneers.

Highlights:

  • Monet's Water Lilies series
  • Renoir's portraits and landscapes
  • Degas' ballet dancer studies
  • Interactive color theory demonstrations
New

Ancient Civilizations

April 10 - October 30, 2025 | Gallery C

Journey through time to explore the artifacts and treasures of ancient Egypt, Greece, Rome, and Mesopotamia. See how these civilizations laid the foundation for modern society.

Highlights:

  • Egyptian mummies and hieroglyphics
  • Greek pottery and sculptures
  • Roman architecture models
  • Mesopotamian cuneiform tablets
