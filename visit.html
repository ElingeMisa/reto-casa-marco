Accessibility

Casa Marco Museum is committed to being accessible to all visitors.

  • Wheelchair accessible entrances and galleries
  • Elevators available to all floors
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Audio guides available
  • Service animals welcome
  • Braille signage throughout
  • ASL interpretation available (by appointment)

Visitor Guidelines

Please Note

  • Photography allowed (no flash)
  • No food or drinks in galleries
  • Large bags must be checked
  • Maintain distance from artworks
  • Quiet voices in galleries
  • Touch only designated interactive exhibits
